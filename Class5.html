    • Important Questions and Answers

    • Question 1:What is Abacus?
    • Answer 1: Abacus is the first mechanical computing device developed by Chinese around 3000 to 4000 years ago. It is made up of wooden rack that has metal rods.
    • Question 2:What is Napier’s bone?
    • Answer 2: Napier’s Bones was manually operated calculating device invented by John Napier. The Napier’s bone consists of eleven sets of rods. Tens rods are carved with multiplication of numbers from 0 to 9 and one rod is carved with the numbers from 1 to 9.
